Cybersecurity domains are the specific fields to protect specific area such as network, personal details, bank details, cloud data and application data.
Some cybersecurity domains are-
1.network security- IN this field we protect network from cyber attacks from unauthorized access from hackers.
2.offensive security- in this field we attack on our server, network, application, and all system to fiind vunerabilitis before real hackers attack.
3.cloud security- in this field we protect cloud data i.e. which is uploaded on the network or say internet from cyber attacks.
4.webapplication security-in this we protect web applications from hackers to protect credential data loss and leak of our users as well as company.
5.defensive security - in this field we defends against attacks by creating firewalls.
6.forensic and incident response team- in this field we investigate and respond to security incidents and cyberattacks.
